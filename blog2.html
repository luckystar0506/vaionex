15 October

By: Vaionex
Metanet Society Bitcoin event.

On the 15th October, we hosted a Metanet society virtual meetup with the Bitcoin association. In the event we talked to Jimmy Nguyen about the Metanet society and the projects we have been working on at Vaionex.

We would like to thank Jimmy Nguyen, the Bitcoin association and all participants for joining us and tuning into this informative seminar.

We touch lightly on our newly developed platforms, presenting: Satolearn, Raspora, ApiAndMe, Relysia and Blockcodes. If you missed the event you can still watch it in the video below.
